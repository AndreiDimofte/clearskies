Density Altitude
Density altitude is pressure altitude corrected for non-standard temperature. High DA means thinner air — less engine power, less lift, longer takeoff roll. Critical for operations at hot, high, or humid airports.

About ClearSkies

Built by aviation enthusiasts,
for pilots.

ClearSkies started as a project to solve a real frustration: aviation data is everywhere, but understanding it quickly is hard. METARs, NOTAMs, TAFs, ATC clearances — all in cryptic shorthand that takes years to decode fluently. ClearSkies makes it instant.

🎯
The Mission
Make aviation data accessible to everyone — from student pilots on their first solo to experienced captains who need a fast briefing. Plain English, structured output, zero confusion.
✈️
The Story
ClearSkies was built out of genuine frustration with how inaccessible aviation data is. Every feature comes from a real problem encountered in real flight training — nothing hypothetical, nothing added for show.
🔬
The Approach
Structured data — wind, visibility, ceiling, QNH — is extracted with precise logic, never guessed. AI is used only where it genuinely helps: narrative summaries, theory explanations, clearance interpretation.
🚀
The Roadmap
Go/No-Go assistant, live METAR trends, shareable briefing links, ATPL exam practice mode, and PWA support for offline use — all actively in development.
